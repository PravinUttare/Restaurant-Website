How to get to Maharaja Indian Restaurant

If travelling with public transpositions (Always the best way…).

  • Bus nr. 36 (Viru-Väike Õismäe) (Väike Õismäe-Viru) Maharaja is located 100m from Rahumäe bus stop. (Viru to Rahumäe 15-18 minutes) (Väike Õismäe to Rahumäe 18-20 minutes)

  • Bus nr. 23 (Bussijaam-Kadaka) (Kadaka-Bussijaam) Maharaja is located 50m from rahumäe bus stop. (Bussijaam to Rahumäe 20-25 minutes)(Kadaka to rahumäe 10-12 minutes)

  • Tallinn Railway station to Rahumäe 5 Minutes journey 4 stops (Train Nr. R11, R12, R13, R14, R15, R16, R31)

  • Travelling by car from City Center to Maharaja Indian Restaurant approx 17 minutes drive, approx 10km.

  • Travelling by car from Mustamäe to Maharaja Indian Restaurant approx 7-8 minutes drive, approx 2-5km.

  • Travelling by car from Väike Õismäe to Maharaja Indian Restaurant approx 11-12 minutes drive, approx 6-8km.

  • Travelling by car from Tallinn Airport to Maharaja Indian Restaurant approx 14-18 minutes drive, approx 11-12km.

  • Travelling by car from Lasnamäe to Maharaja Indian Restaurant approx 14-18 minutes drive, approx 10-13km.

  • Walk from Taltech to Maharaja Indian Restaurant 20-30 minutes.
